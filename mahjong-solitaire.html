Mahjong Solitaire

Experience the classic tile-matching puzzle game with beautiful graphics and relaxing gameplay!

How to Play

Mahjong Solitaire is a classic tile-matching puzzle game! Your goal is to clear all tiles from the board:

  • Find matching pairs of identical mahjong tiles and click to match them
  • Only tiles that are not blocked by other tiles can be selected
  • Tiles must have at least one side (left or right) completely free
  • Flower and season tiles can be matched with any other flower or season tile
  • Clear all tiles to win the game!

  • Beautiful traditional tile designs
  • Multiple layouts and difficulty levels
  • Relaxing sound effects and visual feedback

About Mahjong Solitaire

Mahjong Solitaire is a single-player puzzle game based on traditional Chinese mahjong tiles. The objective is to clear the entire board by matching pairs of identical tiles.

Originally developed as a computer game in the 1980s, it quickly became one of the most popular casual puzzle games. It combines strategic thinking with visual pattern recognition, providing both mental exercise and relaxing entertainment.

Our version features authentic traditional tile designs and multiple game layouts, making this timeless classic enjoyable for players of all ages and skill levels.

Tips & Strategies

For Beginners

  • Focus on clearing top layers and edge tiles first
  • Look for exact matching pairs of identical tiles
  • Use the hint system to learn tile matching patterns
  • Take your time and carefully examine each tile
  • Remember that flower and season tiles can match each other

Advanced Players

  • Prioritize removing tiles that unlock the most other tiles
  • Avoid clearing tiles too early that you might need later
  • Think several moves ahead before making matches
  • Learn to recognize blocked situations and use undo wisely
  • Practice rapid tile pattern recognition for faster play

Tile Types

Number Tiles

  • Characters: 1 through 9
  • Bamboos: 1 through 9
  • Circles: 1 through 9

Honor Tiles

  • Winds: East, South, West, North
  • Dragons: Red, Green, White

Bonus Tiles

  • Flowers: Plum, Orchid, Bamboo, Chrysanthemum
  • Seasons: Spring, Summer, Autumn, Winter
  • ※ Flowers and seasons can match each other
